# PhilippineLead 🇵🇭

A B2B lead generation platform designed specifically for Philippine recruitment agencies and outsourcing companies that need website services.

## Problem Solved

Philippine recruitment and outsourcing companies are a massive market, but many lack online presence. If you're a web developer or digital agency targeting this space, finding and prioritizing qualified leads is time-consuming. **PhilippineLead** automates this by providing a curated, scored, and filterable database of real recruitment companies ready for web services.

## What is PhilippineLead?

PhilippineLead is a full-stack lead generation app that:
- **Identifies** recruitment and outsourcing companies across the Philippines
- **Scores** leads based on location, size, services, and website status
- **Filters** by location, industry, company size, and web presence
- **Prioritizes** high-value prospects with lead scoring (60-100)
- **Exports** data for CRM integration and outreach campaigns
- **Tracks** contact information with clickable email and phone links

## Key Features

🔍 **Smart Lead Scoring** - Automatically ranks leads (60-79 medium, 80+ high priority)  
📍 **Location-Based Filtering** - Search by Manila, Cebu, Davao, Bacolod, and more  
🏢 **Industry & Size Filters** - Target by company type and headcount  
🌐 **Website Status Detection** - Find companies without online presence  
📊 **Professional Lead Cards** - View company details, contact info, and scores at a glance  
📥 **CSV Export** - Download leads for bulk outreach and CRM integration  
☎️ **Direct Contact Links** - Click-to-call and one-click email functionality  
📱 **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile

## How It Works

1. **Browse** - View all leads with company cards showing key information
2. **Filter** - Use location, industry, size, and website status filters
3. **Score** - Leads are automatically ranked by fit and opportunity
4. **Export** - Download qualified leads as CSV for outreach
5. **Outreach** - Use contact links to call or email prospects
